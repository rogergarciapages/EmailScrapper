Dec 21, 2023

Google has finally admitted to quietly placing headlines within the ad copy description text in live ads as part of a “small test.”

But not everyone’s convinced this is a small test – like Google Ads expert Anthony Higman, who told Search Engine Land: “Everyone on my team and other people in the PPC community are seeing this in live ads. So it seems like larger tests are taking place.”

If this test is rolled out on a larger scale as anticipated, it could prove problematic for advertisers with smaller budgets without the capacity to monitor the impact of ad variations. This headache coupled with a lack of transparency isn’t exactly the best gift Google could have given marketers for Christmas. Just saying!
